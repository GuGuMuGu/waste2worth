Waste2Worth

Transforming Community Waste into Sustainable Value

About the Venture

Waste2Worth is a community-driven waste management initiative designed to tackle environmental pollution by educating households, promoting segregation, and enabling recycling and composting practices in rural and semi-urban areas.

How It Works

1. Educate

Raise awareness about waste segregation and the importance of sustainable waste practices.

2. Collect

Implement local collection points and micro waste centers in communities.

3. Transform

Convert organic waste into compost and recyclable materials into useful products.
